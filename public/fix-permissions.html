Method 2: Chrome Settings

  1. Copy and paste this into your address bar: chrome://settings/content/camera
  2. Press Enter
  3. Look for this website (localhost:8000 or your domain) in the "Block" section
  4. Click the trash/delete icon next to it to remove it

  6. Go back to the video chat and try again

Method 3: Clear Site Data

  1. Right-click anywhere on the video chat page
  2. Select "Inspect" or "Inspect Element"
  3. Click the "Application" tab at the top
  4. In the left sidebar, click "Storage"
  5. Click the "Clear site data" button
  6. Close the developer tools and refresh the page
  7. When prompted for camera/microphone access, click "Allow"
